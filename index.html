CHAPMAN'S ICE CREAM REBRAND

Over the course of five weeks, I completed a redesign for Chapman’s Ice Cream, focusing on modernizing its logo, packaging, and overall visual identity. This process involved refining the brand’s look and feel through design choices that emphasize a fresh, youthful energy while maintaining brand recognition. Throughout the redesign, I worked with Adobe Express, Adobe Illustrator, and Adobe Photoshop to create high-quality visuals that are both engaging and cohesive. Additionally, I conceptualized, filmed,and edited a series of social media videos to promote the updated branding. Links to these videos can be found below.

5 DRIVE-IN WEBSITE
REDESIGN

For the drive-in website redesign, I updated key sections,
including the menu, landing page, movie display, and
information page, to create a cohesive, user-friendly experience.
Starting with low-fidelity sitemaps in Adobe Illustrator, I
restructured each area to improve navigation and accessibility.
I then developed high-fidelity mockups using Figma, integrating
a refreshed colour palette, modern typography, and updated
layouts. These updates bring a lively, engaging feel to the
site, ensuring users can easily find showtimes, book tickets,
and access essential information easily.This project was part of
my second-year work in the GBDA 228 HTML coding class,
where I applied design principles to develop a user friendly
and visually appealing website.
