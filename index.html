CHAPMAN'S ICE CREAM
REBRAND

Over the course of five weeks, I completed a redesign for Chapman’s Ice Cream, focusing on modernizing its logo, packaging, and overall visual identity. This process involved refining the brand’s look and feel through design choices that emphasize a fresh, youthful energy while maintaining brand recognition. Throughout the redesign, I worked with Adobe Express, Adobe Illustrator, and Adobe Photoshop to create high-quality visuals that are both engaging and cohesive. Additionally, I conceptualized, filmed,and edited a series of social media videos to promote the updated branding. Links to these videos can be found below.
